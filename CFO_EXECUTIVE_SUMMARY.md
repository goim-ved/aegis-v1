## 1. Product Overview
We have successfully built **PROXY**, a next-generation financial infrastructure system. Think of it as a digital "Compliance Guardrail" that sits between our business operations and the blockchain.

**What it does:**
It allows us to onboard legal entities (companies, individuals) and issue them a secure, digital identity badge ("Soulbound Token") that proves they are compliant. This badge cannot be sold or transferred, ensuring we always know who we are dealing with.
